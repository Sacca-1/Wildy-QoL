# Wildy QoL

PvP and Wilderness tools for preventing misclicks, checking your setup, improving timers, saving prayer layouts, and more.

## Features

### Misclick prevention

- Block left-clicks on rune pouches and empty vials in PvP areas while keeping their right-click options.
- Remove Cast and Examine options from pets while a spell is selected.
- Block special attack orb clicks in PvP areas or everywhere.
- Show marlin as anglerfish and halibut as karambwan in PvP areas to make similar food easier to tell apart.

### PvP setup warnings

- Remind you when Protect Item is not active in a PvP area.
- Check supported ranged weapons for missing, low, incompatible, or suboptimal ammo.
- Check supported charged items for empty, low, or unknown charges.
- Check your spellbook and runes when you have no supported casts, low cast counts, or a mismatched setup.
- Check that you have a supported teleport out for Wilderness level 20 or 30.

Text overlay warnings can appear at banks, supported PvP banks, in PvP areas, or everywhere. You can also limit these warnings to skulled characters and set each low-amount warning separately.

Charge tracking for tomes, the toxic staff of the dead, and the serpentine helm is inspired by TicTac7x's Item Charges Improved plugin.

### Extended freeze timers

- Account for freeze-length bonuses from Ancient sceptres, Zuriel's staves, and Swampbark.
- Keep freeze timers running after forced movement from seeds or dragon spear and hasta special attacks.
- Optionally turn off RuneLite's own freeze timer when both timers are enabled.

### Prayer layouts

- Save reordered and hidden prayers for each character and supported LMS or PvP Arena build.
- Restore the matching layout when you switch character or build.
- Optionally show a restore message and hide your RSN in it.

This feature requires prayer reordering in RuneLite's Prayer plugin.

### Menaphite proc timer

- Track when your next menaphite remedy proc can trigger.
- Show the countdown in an info box, beside the inventory, or both.
- Display the countdown in seconds or game ticks.

### DMM overload proc timer

- Track when your next DMM overload proc can trigger.
- Show the countdown in an info box, beside the inventory, or both.
- Display the countdown in seconds or game ticks.

### PvP Arena

- Remove tall scenery that can block your view during fights.
- Highlight spellbooks that do not match your chosen rule.

### Items Kept on Death

- Add repair costs for broken and mangled untradeables to the risk total.
